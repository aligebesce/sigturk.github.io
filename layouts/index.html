Comprehensive

SIGTURK Core ensures that participants can contribute alternative perspectives with the imminent or potential computational benefit. This principle allows SIGTURK to welcome more people and entities into its activities.

Formal

If SIGTURK defines, the participants do. Otherwise not. For traceable input and output, SIGTURK follows a model of an open-source, open-license, open-contribution core specification built with a consensus process.

Inclusive

SIGTURK focuses on discussions that focus on the minimal effort to produce maximal benefit computational artifacts. Hence, attacking, bullying, doxing, discrimination, harassing, and insulting are not welcome.

Diverse

SIGTURK is a place for all demographics from all backgrounds and identities to contribute their enthusiasm towards building a constructive dialogue for producing reusable artifacts.
